Description

I created this website to help people when packing for a trip. I can't tell you how many times I've looked online for pre-packing lists and then I forget something that I had bought specifically for this that wasn't referenced in the list. I frequently forget items, even if I start my list several days in advance. And since I take similar items on similar trips, I always thought it would be cool to have multiple lists saved that had all of the items that I might want to bring for my rock climbing trip or for my scuba diving trip. I have lists for road trips vs plane. This has saved so much time on my part and I really hope that it saves you time and stress as well on your next vacation.
